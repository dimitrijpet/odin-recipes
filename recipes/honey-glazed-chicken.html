Honey Glazed Chicken

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey

  • tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. hot and enjoy!
